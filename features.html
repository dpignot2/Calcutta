🎯

Vegas Late-Round Anchoring

Vegas only gives a championship number. Instead of spreading it evenly, 56% of the adjustment concentrates in F4/Championship where the real uncertainty lives. R64 barely moves.

🔄

Graceful Degradation

Have all three sources? Full ensemble. Only Barttorvik? Bracket model still works. The dashboard adapts blending weights to whatever data you have.

📊

Bayesian Pot Estimation

Each sale implies a pot size based on that team's EV share. 1-seed sales give more signal than 16-seeds. 90% credible interval tightens as the auction progresses.

📋 KenPom Profile Intelligence
Five data-driven checks that separate real contenders from paper tigers. Based on 20+ years of tournament champion profiles.
🏆

Championship Profile

Top 25 in both offense AND defense. 22 of 23 champions since 2002 had this. Teams without it get penalized up to −15%.

+5% if qualified −15% if major imbalance
📐

Trapezoid of Excellence

High efficiency at moderate pace. Champions cluster here — extreme-tempo teams are more volatile and matchup-dependent.

+3% inside −5% extreme pace
🍀

Luck Discount

Teams winning close games at unsustainable rates. Their record looks better than they are. Regression is coming.

−10% very lucky +5% unlucky (undervalued)
⚖️

Balance Check

Both O and D rank in top 40 = tournament-proof. Can win shootouts AND grind-it-out games. One-dimensional teams get flagged.

+2% balanced −8% extremely lopsided
🔀

Source Disagreement

When Barttorvik and KenPom disagree by 3+ AdjEM points or 20+ rank spots, the team is flagged for investigation. One model sees something the other doesn't.

⚠️ Flagged — no auto-adjust

🎁 Bonus Payouts
Two bonus models that can transform a $3 team into a $17 value play.
😵

Biggest Blowout (Consolation Prize)

Goes to the team that LOSES by the largest margin in Round of 64. Uses softmax over expected margin² across all 32 games. 16-seeds dominate — Wagner gets 27% blowout probability ($14 bonus EV) vs $0.05 from round payouts.

👩

Women's Championship

Automatically converts women's futures odds to de-vigged win probabilities and matches to men's bracket teams by school. South Carolina adds $29 bonus EV. Most schools: $0. A few contenders: $3–17.
